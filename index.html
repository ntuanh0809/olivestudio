OLIVE STUDIO - TOP BAKESHOP IN HANOI
Olive Studio was founded in 2016 by a passionate team. We love to create and bake good treats for family, friends and customers to enjoy. It is all a long process from finding the right recipe, the right ingredients to the finished products to serve. Our customised and baked treats for any occasion are baked fresh daily, using only the best ingredients we can find so we can make sure that the cake you would receive is at its best.
